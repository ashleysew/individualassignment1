Reflection on Personality Trait Test & Results

The 16Personalities test evaluates its respondents through a series of meaningful and objective questions. The respondents are able to answer to what degree they agree or disagree with the statement. This aspect allows the test to generate a true or accurate reading of an individual. The respondent’s answers aren’t limited to only two or three possible responses which support the categorized results generated in five (5) traits. This showcases the validity of the test as the personality type assigned is supported by the scored percentages of five (5) personality traits. In my case, in particular, the character traits, strengths and weaknesses, friendships, and career path described under the Advocate personality type align with the type of person that I am today. It made mention of some traits which weren’t specifically within the line of questions. My scored results in all five (5) traits showcased the introverted, intuitive, emotional, orderly, and self-conscious perfectionist I am. I believe that the test truly displayed the type of person I am.
